Electronics Recycling

Getting rid of your old electronics is free and simple.

Drop off location

The Electronics Recycling Center 
Lexington-Fayette Urban County Government Campus’ warehouse
1306 Versailles Road
Lexington, KY 40504

Follow the posted signs to the next to last building on the property. Drive into the building and your items will be unloaded for you.

Hours of operation

  • Monday, Tuesday, Thursday and Friday: 8 a.m. – 4 p.m.
  • Wednesday: Noon – 4 p.m.
  • Saturday: 8 a.m. – noon.
  • We are closed on government holidays.

Accepted items

  • Computers, printers, copiers
  • Printer cartridges
  • Toner cases
  • Handheld games
  • Cell phones and other communication devices
  • Microwave ovens
  • Televisions and monitors
  • CDs, DVDs and their cases
  • VHS and cassette tapes
  • Audio and video players/equipment
  • Small corded electrical items (such as toasters, hair dryers, or radios)
  • Holiday lights and extension cords
  • Fluorescent light tubes (no CFLs – take those to Lowe’s or Home Depot)
  • MP3 players, PDAs, laptops and electronic tablets
  • Rechargeable batteries (We do not accept automotive/lead-acid or household alkaline batteries)
