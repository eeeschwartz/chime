Trash, Recycling and Yard Waste Pickup Day

Landfill waste, recycling and yard waste are collected once a week on the same day. Be aware that anything within a 3-foot radius of the Herbie shall be deemed garbage to be collected.

All carts, including the Herbie, Rosie and Lenny, should be placed on the curb:

  • no earlier than 4 p.m. on the day before collection
  • no later than 5 a.m. on the day of your collection

Carts must be removed from the curb by 7 p.m. on the day of your collection.
